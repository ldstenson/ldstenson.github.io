Hi! I'm Lilliana Stenson.

I'm a Junior at UW-Madison majoring in Information Science, and I'm a big fan of video games.
This website's purpose is to display some of my favorite games of all time and try to convince you to play them. I'm partial to RPGs, but I'm open-minded to anything with a compelling story, fleshed-out characters, fun movement abilities, or good gunplay.
